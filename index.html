ABOUT

Ramen is a popular dish among those in the Rembau area who want something good and simple to prepare,

and ServeMi is a cafe that was established in response to this desire. Our business is focused on

the people in Pekan Rembau or more specifically the children and teenagers who are not over 30 years old

because at this age they still want to explore new foods other than just western food. We chose the Pekan

Rembau area because this area is the focus of the people around Rembau therefore, we came up with the idea

of opening a "ServeMi Cafe” and our product specialist in the list “Lamyeon Chingu” to make it special for

our brand. We chose ServeMi Cafe because we want to look aesthetically pleasing and have a Korean vibe to it

and show our customers that we serve mee or noodles to them. We also prioritize the taste of the community in

Rembau which is popular with the "Daging Salai '' dish. We also put it as a special menu for those who want

something different in ramen dishes so that we can attract customers to try this menu.


“VISION”

To become the best product provider to the Rembau community.




“MISSION”

Giving excellent quality service and products to the customers.
